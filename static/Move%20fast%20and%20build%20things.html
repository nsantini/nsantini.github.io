Move fast and build things

12th September 2021 at 4:25pm
post

We are in the age of APIs. That’s what came after SAAS. Nowadays you can put together a whole new value proposition in record time by utilising publicly available services.

These new services are not an expensive one stop shop (SAAS), but a collection of compost Le APIs. Think of Twilio, PayPal, AWS, etc.

By picking the right modular services one can assemble a new product in record time and at a very affordable price.

There is not time like the present to start a new venture. Today is easier than ever to move fast and build things!
